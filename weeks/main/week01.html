Week 1: Introduction, Workshop Safety & Constraints

Introduction

In Week 1 of the Mirror unit, we set the stage for a successful project by reviewing essential workshop safety and discussing design constraints. Establishing a safety-first mindset and understanding project limitations (materials, time, tools) will guide all our work.

Workshop Safety Refresher

  • Always obey WHS signage (Warning, Mandatory, Danger, etc.) and workshop rules learned in previous units. These signs use standardised colours and symbols to convey hazards and required actions.
