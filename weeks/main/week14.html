Week 14: Fitting the Glass & Industry Connection

Introduction

As we approach the project’s end, Week 14 deals with the critical step of fitting the glass into your finished frame. We’ll cover techniques for safely installing the mirror glass and link classroom learning with industry practices through an industry visit or virtual tour.

Fitting the Mirror Glass

Important considerations include:

  • Handling Glass Safely: Wear gloves and handle carefully to avoid chipping.
  • Cleaning: Clean the glass thoroughly before installation.
  • Inserting: Place the glass into the rebate gently without forcing.
  • Securing the Glass: Use mirror retaining clips, glazier’s points, or other methods to hold the glass securely.
  • Padding (Optional): Add felt or a rubber gasket if the glass is loose.
  • Backing: Optionally, install a backing board to protect the glass.

Industry Link: Workshop in Action

  • If visiting: Tour a local joinery workshop or furniture manufacturer.
  • If virtual: Watch a video tour or join a live session with a tradesperson discussing real-world practices.

Student Activity

After fitting your mirror, take a photo of the completed piece (front view) and upload it to the Project Showcase on Classroom. Then, describe one interesting aspect of the industry visit or video.
