Week 19: Project Catch-Up & Workshop Clean-Up

Introduction

Week 19 is a catch-up week and workshop clean-up session. This time is used for finishing any project tasks, completing portfolios, and thoroughly cleaning and organizing the workshop.

Catch-Up Activities

Activities may include:

  • Project Fixes: Make any necessary repairs or refinements.
  • Portfolio Completion: Finalize and submit your project portfolio.
  • Make-Up Tests/Modules: Complete any missed assessments or safety tests.
  • Enrichment: Engage in fun tasks like creating a jig or practicing an extra skill.

Workshop Clean-Up

The class will work in teams to:

  • Tool Inventory & Storage: Return tools to their proper places and report any issues.
  • Machine Maintenance: Clean machines and check for sharpness or needed repairs.
  • Material Storage: Organize lumber racks and clear out unusable scrap.
  • Benches and Vices: Clean work surfaces and equipment.
  • Floor and Corners: Sweep and vacuum to remove dust and debris.
  • Finishing Area: Ensure finish cans and cleaning supplies are properly stored.

Student Activity

Your activity this week is hands-on: complete your assigned cleaning checklist and work as a team to leave the workshop spotless.

Conclusion

Week 19 reinforces the importance of a clean, organized workspace in a professional setting. Your efforts in cleaning now will benefit everyone in future projects.
