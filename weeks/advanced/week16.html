Polishing the Finished Mirror Frame

Your finish should be fully cured. Enhance its look by:

  • Wax Application: Apply a thin layer of furniture wax with a soft cloth.
  • Buffing: Rub the waxed surface with a clean cloth or buffing pad to create a soft sheen.
  • Alternatives: For a high gloss, wet sand and polish lightly with an automotive rubbing compound, then wax.
  • Ensure the finish is completely cured before polishing to avoid scratches.

Final Hardware and Checks

Attach any hanging hardware (D-rings, sawtooth brackets, etc.) and do a final check to ensure all parts are secure and the finish is even.
