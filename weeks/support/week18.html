Week 18: Theory Class Test & Feedback

Introduction

Week 18 is dedicated to the Class Test on theory topics from the term and reviewing results and feedback. This test will cover all key concepts from workshop safety to timber finishing.

Class Test Overview

The test may include:

  • Multiple Choice & True/False: Identifying the correct tools, safety symbols, and timber characteristics.
  • Short Answer: Listing PPE, describing safe storage methods, explaining seasoning, etc.
  • Diagram Labeling: Identifying parts of tools or timber cross-sections.
  • Application Questions: Explaining how to resolve common issues like a rough surface on wood.

During the Test

Follow test instructions carefully. Use your practical experiences to guide your answers and manage your time well.

Post-Test Review

After the test, we will review the answers together and discuss common mistakes. This immediate feedback reinforces your learning.

Feedback on Projects

Your project portfolios and evaluations will be returned with feedback. Use this constructive criticism to improve your future work.
